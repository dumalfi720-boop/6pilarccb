💙 Skill: Lead Research

A lead research skill that confirms niche, researches prospects, classifies by temperature (Hot/Warm/Cold), deduplicates and exports in CSV ready for CRM. Commercial automation with AI.

🎯 Purpose of the Skill

The Lead Research skill automates the lead research processidentify, qualify and organize leadsfor commercial teams. Instead of spending hours manually searching Google and LinkedIn, you define the niche and the skill does the heavy lifting.

🎯Main Concept

# Purpose
Pesquisar e qualificar leads para o nicho especificado em $ARGUMENTS.
Classificar cada lead por temperatura (Hot/Warm/Cold) baseado em
sinais de compra. Exportar lista final deduplicada em formato CSV
pronto para importacao em CRM (HubSpot, Pipedrive, etc).

💡Practical Tip

This skill works best with a web search MCP (like Brave Search or Tavily) to search real data. Without search MCP, Claude works with his own knowledge, which may be out of date. Combine skill + MCP for fresh results.

TOPIC 2
2

🔎 Niche Confirmation

The first step of the skill andconfirm and refine the nichebefore starting the search. This avoids wasting tokens searching for leads that do not fit the ICP (Ideal Customer Profile).

🎯Main Concept

# Step 1: Niche Confirmation
1. Analisar o nicho fornecido em $ARGUMENTS
2. Definir ICP (Ideal Customer Profile):
   - Segmento: B2B ou B2C
   - Tamanho da empresa: startup/PME/enterprise
   - Localizacao: cidade/estado/pais
   - Cargo do decisor: CEO/CTO/CMO/Head of...
3. Confirmar com o usuario antes de prosseguir
4. Se nicho muito amplo, sugerir segmentacao

💡Practical Tip

The more specific the niche, the better the quality of the leads. "Digital marketing agencies in SP with 10-50 employees" generates much more useful leads than "marketing agencies". The time invested in defining the ICP pays off 10x in the quality of the output.

TOPIC 3
3

🔬 Research Method

The skill uses alayered search strategy: Start broad to map the market, then filter and validate each lead individually.

🎯Main Concept

  • •Layer 1 - Mapping:List niche companies via web search, directories and associations
  • •Layer 2 – Enrichment:For each company, find website, social network, decision maker, default email
  • •Layer 3 - Signals:Check buying signals (hiring, raised capital, launched product, posted about pain)

💡Practical Tip

Set a lead limit per run (ex: 20-30). More than that and the quality drops because Claude starts to "invent" data. Better to run the skill 3 times with 20 leads than once with 60.

TOPIC 4
4

🌡️ Hot/Warm/Cold Classification

Not all leads are the same. The skill classifies each one bytemperature based on objective signals, so that the commercial team prioritizes those most likely to convert.

🎯Main Concept

TemperatureCriteriaAction
HOT 🔥Posted about the problem, hiring in the area, asked for a solution recommendationContact in 24h
WARM ☀️Company in the right niche, adequate size, identified decision makerContact in 1 week
COLD ❄️In niche but no buying signals, incomplete dataNourish with content

💡Practical Tip

Customize temperature criteria for your business. What is "hot" for a B2B SaaS (raised Series A) is different from "hot" for a local agency (posted needing the service). Adapt the signals in the .md of the skill.

TOPIC 5
5

🔄 Deduplication

When you run the skill multiple times or combine results from different sources,duplicates are inevitable. The skill includes a deduplication step that unifies records and preserves the most complete data.

🎯Main Concept

  • •Match by domain:Same website = same company, merge data
  • •Match by name:Fuzzy matching for variations (Ltda, SA, Inc)
  • •Data conflict:Maintain the most complete and recent record
  • •Report:Report how many duplicates found and removed

💡Practical Tip

If you already have a list in CRM, pass it as additional input: "Deduplicate against this existing list: [file.csv]". This avoids rework and ensures that the skill only brings truly new leads.

TOPIC 6
6

📊 Output CSV

The end result is astructured CSV fileready to import into any CRM. Standardized columns, correct encoding, and format compatible with HubSpot, Pipedrive, Salesforce and Google Sheets.

🎯Main Concept

# Output Format: CSV
Colunas:
company_name, website, industry, size, city, state,
contact_name, contact_title, contact_email, contact_linkedin,
temperature, signal, notes, source, date_found

Encoding: UTF-8 com BOM (compatibilidade Excel)
Separador: virgula
Aspas: usar quando campo contem virgula ou quebra de linha

💡Practical Tip

Adapt the CSV columns to your CRM fields before using the skill. There's nothing worse than importing 100 leads and discovering that the "contact_title" field in the CSV is called "job_title" in your HubSpot. Prior alignment saves rework.
